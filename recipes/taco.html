Taco Salad

Home

Description

The taco salad is made with taco-seasoned ground lamb, chilli beans, and tomatoes combined with cheese and crunchy tortilla chips for a tasty twist on regular taco salad. The children have fun preparing all the extras before we mix it all together. Serve on lettuce and top with salsa and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Lamb: The meaty taco salad begins with a pound of lean ground lamb
  • Beans: You will also need a can of chilli beans
  • Dressing: Use store-bought or homemade French salad dressing
  • Seasoning: Use store-bought or homemade taco seasoning mix
  • Chips: Crushed tortilla chips add welcome crunch and salty flavour
  • lettuce: You cannot have without shredded iceberg lettuce
  • Toppings: Top your taco salad with Cheddar cheese, tomatoes, salsa and sour cream

Procedure

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this easy taco salad recipes

  1. Cook the lamb, then stir in the beans, dressing, and seasoning.
  2. Place crushed chips in a bowl, then top with lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes.
  3. Mix in the meat mixture.
  4. Finish the taco salad bowls with sour cream and salsa.
