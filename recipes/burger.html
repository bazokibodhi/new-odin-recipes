Natasha's Chicken Burger

Home

Description

A chicken burger the whole family enjoys! Natasha is my eldest daughter and her eyes light when I announce we're having these for dinner. The onion can be finely chopped, but with three kids we prefer grating so as to hide it. I usually serve these with mashed potatoes and Caeser salad for a very comforting meal!!

Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you'll need to make this chicken burger recipe:

  • Ground chicken: This recipe starts with a pound of extra lean ground chicken
  • Bread Crumbs: Use store-bought bread-crumbs or make at your own home
  • Onion: Half an onion lends texture and flavour
  • Egg: An egg adds moisture and helps bind the chicken patties together
  • Seasonings: These chicken burgers are seasoned with fresh garlic, salt, and pepper
  • Oil: Cook the chicken burger patties in olive oil on the stove

How to make

  1. Mix the chicken, half of the crumbs, onion, egg, and seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into oval-shaped patties.
  3. Coat the patties into the remaining bread crumbs.
  4. Cook the patties until they are deeply brown on both the sides.
